About Ganeshotsav

Ganeshotsav is a ten-day Hindu festival celebrated to honor Lord Ganesha, the God of wisdom, prosperity, and good fortune. The Windows Society is proud to organize this grand celebration bringing together our community in a spirit of unity and devotion.

This year's celebration will feature various cultural events, competitions, and spiritual activities that showcase our rich heritage and bring the community together from August 27th to September 6th, 2025.
